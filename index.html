WE'RE THE ROCKIN' BOTS

We're FIRST Robotics team 2035, the Robo-Rockin' Bots, from Carmel, California! Our team began in 2007, and has been spreading enthusiasm for STEM throughout the Monterey Peninsula ever since. We participate in FIRST Robotics Competitions on the state and national level, and our mission is to design innovative robots and promote STEM throughtout our community and others.
